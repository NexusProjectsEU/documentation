## How to Earn Badges

Most badges are obtainable through active community involvement and valuable contributions. Here's how you can work towards earning them:

- **Stay active** in the community
- **Help other users** with their questions
- **Contribute** to documentation and resources
- **Provide feedback** to help us improve
- **Share your knowledge** and experience with ProteX
- **Apply for Staff** by following our [Supporter Application Guide](/docs/guides/supporter)

Ready to get involved? Join our community and start contributing today!
