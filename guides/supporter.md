# Supporter Application Guide

If you want to become a Supporter, you can submit an application through our appeal system. Our team will review your application and determine if you meet the requirements to join the Support team.

---

## 1. Accessing the Application System

1. **Go to the Supporter application link** shared in the server or announcement
2. **Login with Discord**
   - Click the "Login with Discord" button
3. **Authorize access**
   - This verifies your identity and ensures only valid users apply

---

## 2. Filling Out the Supporter Application Form

After logging in, you will see a form with the following fields:

### Discord ID (Required)
- Enter your Discord account ID
- This identifies who you are applying as

> *Tip:* You can get your Discord ID by enabling Developer Mode → Right-click your profile → **Copy ID**

### Why do you want to become a Supporter? (Required)

Explain clearly:

- Why you want to join the Support team
- What motivates you to help the community
- What you hope to do as a Supporter

**Good points to include**
- Desire to help users
- Interest in improving the community
- Responsible and mature attitude

### Why should we accept you? (Required)

This is the most important part. You should include:

- Your experience helping others
- Your knowledge of rules and community standards
- Examples of responsible and positive behavior
- Skills you have that are useful for Support (communication, patience, activity)

### Availability & Activity (Required)
Tell us:

- How often you are active
- Time zones you are available
- How frequently you can assist users

---

## 3. Submitting Your Application

1. Review your answers for accuracy and professionalism
2. Click **Submit**
3. Wait for the confirmation message

---

## 4. After Submission

### What happens next?
- Your application will be reviewed by staff
- Response time: **Typically 24–72 hours**
- You will be notified in Discord or through the system

### Possible Results

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Accepted** | You are approved and will receive Supporter onboarding |
| **Denied** | Your application was not accepted — you may try again later |

---

## 5. Best Practices for Applications

### ✅ Do
- ✅ Be mature and respectful
- ✅ Explain your motivation clearly
- ✅ Show understanding of rules and behavior standards
- ✅ Provide real examples of helping and positive conduct

### ❌ Don’t
- ❌ Rush your answers
- ❌ Copy someone else’s application
- ❌ Beg for the role or guilt-trip staff
- ❌ Submit troll responses or joke answers

---

## 6. Requirements & Expectations

### Minimum Expectations
- Positive community behavior
- Active in chat and helpful attitude
- Knowledge of server rules
- Good communication skills

### Important Notes
- Support role is trusted — misuse will lead to removal
- You must stay mature, calm, and respectful at all times

---

## 7. Example Application

**Why do you want to become a Supporter?**  
"I want to help users who have questions and provide support for the community. I enjoy assisting people and want to contribute to a positive and friendly environment."

**Why should we accept you?**  
"I have been active in the server and regularly help users with questions. I understand the rules well and always stay respectful. I am patient, good at explaining things, and can contribute consistently."

**Availability & Activity**  
"I am online daily for several hours and can assist at different times throughout the day."
