# Supporter Application Guide

If you are interested in helping users, assisting staff, and contributing to a safe and organized community, you can apply to become a Supporter. This guide explains how to correctly fill out the Supporter application.

---

## 1. Requirements

Before applying, make sure you meet the following requirements:

- You are **15 years or older**
- You are **active on Discord**
- You can communicate clearly and respectfully
- You understand this is a **volunteer position**
- You are familiar with Protex staff rules and guidelines

If you meet these requirements, continue to the form.

---

## 2. Accessing the Application

1. **Navigate to appeal.gg**: Go to [appeal.gg/cBp3tee8hJ](https://appeal.gg/cBp3tee8hJ) in your browser
2. **Login with Discord**: Click the "Login with Discord" button
3. **Authorize permissions**: Allow appeal.gg to access your Discord account to verify your identity

---

## 3. Filling Out the Application

After logging in, you will see several fields:

### Timezone (Required)
- Enter your timezone (e.g., **GMT+1**, **EST**, **PST**)
- This helps with scheduling and staff coverage

> **Tip:** Use a standard timezone format for clarity.

---

### Why do you want to become a Supporter? (Required)

Explain your motivation, including:

- Why you want to help the community
- What interests you about the Supporter role
- What positive impact you plan to make

**Good things to include:**
- Desire to help users
- Interest in moderation or community support
- Understanding of community needs and rules

---

### Do you have any experience with support, moderation, or community management?

If yes, briefly explain:

- Previous moderation roles
- Customer support or volunteer experience
- Any leadership or community positions

> **No experience?**  
Write that you are eager to learn — honesty is better than pretending.

---

### Confirmation Checklist (Required)

You must confirm the following:

- ✅ I am at least 15 years old  
- ✅ I am active on Discord  
- ✅ I understand this is a volunteer position  
- ✅ I agree to follow the Protex staff rules and guidelines

You cannot apply without acknowledging these.

---

## 4. Submitting the Application

1. Review your answers for professionalism
2. Ensure there are no jokes, trolling, or incomplete responses
3. Click **Submit**

You will receive a confirmation when the application is submitted.

---

## 5. After Submission

### Review Process
- Applications are reviewed by staff
- Response time: **24–72 hours**
- You will be notified via Discord

### Decisions

| Result | Meaning |
|--------|--------|
| **Accepted** | You will receive onboarding and next steps |
| **Declined** | You may apply again later after improving |

---

## 6. Tips for a Strong Application

### ✅ Do
- Be respectful and mature
- Write complete and thoughtful answers
- Demonstrate genuine interest in helping
- Mention helpful behavior you already show in the server

### ❌ Don’t
- Joke or troll in your answers
- Rush through the form
- Beg or guilt staff
- Write “idk” or copy someone else’s answers

---

## 7. Example Application Answers

"I enjoy helping people and want to contribute to the community. I am active every day and often answer questions in chat, so I would like to do it officially and support the team."
